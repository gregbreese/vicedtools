vicedtools.acer.patresults module¶

Classes for storing data from PAT group reports exported from OARS.

The class PATResults stores collated results for a particular PAT test/ test number.

The class PATResultsCollection stores PATResults and organises them by their test/test number. Includes the ability to take a folder containing all of your group report exports and to parse them all in one go.
